What I Do

Battery Pack Design

I design custom lithium-ion battery packs tailored for specialized applications. Customers provide their requirements, and I deliver battery packs built to their exact specifications complete with integrated safety systems and protective measures.

PCB Development

I develop custom printed circuit boards (PCBs) and manage their fabrication through reliable online platforms that manufacture in China. These PCBs are used in a wide range of applications, including control systems, power management, and testing equipment. My designs primarily utilize surface-mount technology (SMT), with through-hole components used as needed for connectors or custom parts.

Custom Tools

I also acquire custom or specialized tools as needed for each project. This may include electronic load testers, spot welders, reflow ovens, power supplies, and various measurement instruments all of which support the successful completion of client work or personal project development.
